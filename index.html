The Asia Pacific Information Network is a network of people who use data to inform evidence-based decision-making across the Red Cross and Red Crescent.

APIN includes IM practitioners, PMER, DRR, IT and operations people of the Red Cross and Red Crescent (RCRC) movement in Asia Pacific.

APIN aims to enable people across the region to connect, collaborate, share experiences and knowledge, and discuss data and information related to RCRC programs.

The network

APIN is open to Red Cross/Red Crescent people from across the Asia Pacific region. We have regular virtual meetings/events (at least once per month), and communicate in-between-times via Slack, email and WhatsApp.
